Dr. Universe is an annual competition for women with doctorates who have significant lifetime achievements and are the epitome of intelligence, elegance, inner beauty, and a philanthropic spirit.

Acceptance of this achievement award entails a commitment to educating the public in one's chosen doctorate field for a period of 1 year in order to retain the title. Details are in the application.

Scoring: 25% Interview, 15% Inspirational Speech, 10% CV,
25% Evening Gown, 10% Beauty & Poise, 15% Public Presence
